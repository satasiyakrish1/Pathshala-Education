Place your brand assets in this folder:

📁 public/
  ├── logo.png          ← Main logo (recommended: transparent PNG, min 400px wide)
  ├── logo-dark.png     ← Dark version of logo (optional)
  ├── favicon.ico       ← Browser tab icon (32x32 or 64x64)
  ├── hero-image.jpg    ← Hero section classroom/school photo
  ├── about-image.jpg   ← About/mission section photo
  └── og-image.jpg      ← Social share image (1200x630px)

All images placed here are accessible via "/" in the app.
Example: <img src="/logo.png" /> or in CSS: url('/hero-image.jpg')
